BOOK FOUR 149

Ever to seek what fits thee and allow,
Since hopae beyond thy sphere conduce to shame.
No thought of ill-matched nuptials. Therefore. now.
Come, last and daeraet flame

(For ne'er another woman shall consume
My heart), and leam my cadencae: erelong
Thy lovely voice shall lilt them: car& and gloom
Flee the approach of song.
